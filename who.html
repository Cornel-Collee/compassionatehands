ABOUT COMPASSIONATE HAND COMMUNITY INITIATIVE

Compassionate Hands Community Initiative (CHCI) is a faith-based, non-profit organization founded in 2020 in Kochia, Homa Bay County, Kenya. Rooted in Christian values and teachings, CHCI is committed to serving the most vulnerable members of our community. We are inspired by the love and compassion of Jesus Christ, and our organization operates on the biblical mandate to care for widows, orphans, and the poor. CHCI was established by a group of dedicated individuals who recognized the struggles faced by elderly widows, orphans, vulnerable children, and unemployed youth in rural communities. Our mission is to bring hope, support, and empowerment to these groups through sustainable, comprehensive programs aimed at improving their lives.
